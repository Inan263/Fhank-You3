Barcelona Present Players

The 2024–25 Futbol Club Barcelona season is the club's 125th season in existence and their 94th consecutive season in the top flight. In addition to the domestic league, Barcelona will be participating in this season's editions of the Copa del Rey, the Supercopa de España and the UEFA Champions League (entering for the 21st consecutive season). The season covers the period from 1 July 2024 until 30 June 2025. Barcelona will play their official home matches at the Estadi Olímpic Lluís Companys until 15 December 2024, when it is expected to return to the Camp Nou still under-renovation works and with no full seating capacity available.
